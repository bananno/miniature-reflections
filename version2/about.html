About Us
With 40 years of combined sewing experience, Dori and Jill love creating with fabric! Currently working as costumers for a regional non-profit ballet company, they have designed a line of costumes for 18" dolls which are replicas of the actual Nutcracker ballet costumes worn by the dancers on the stage. Using many of the same materials and authentic techniques, these tiny tutus, dresses and tiaras are miniature reflections of a classic art form. Designed as a fundraiser, portion of these sales is donated to the Gainesville Ballet Company.
